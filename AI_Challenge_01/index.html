How the recommendation is calculated

The recommended plan balances practical coverage depth with price. Annual limits, included benefit categories, lower copay, and shorter waiting periods all improve the score, while a high monthly premium reduces it. This keeps the recommendation focused on value for money, not only the most expensive plan.
